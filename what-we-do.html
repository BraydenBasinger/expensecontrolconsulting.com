What We Do

We guarantee you savings, or we don't get paid

We take a hard look at the bills you are already paying. We find the overcharges, the errors, and the fees that should not be there. Then we win that money back and keep your costs low going forward. You do not pay us unless we save you money.

Our Services

A few categories we help with

Uniforms

The big uniform rental companies make their invoices hard to read. We look for rate hikes that climb way past inflation, charges for items you never lost or damaged, and fees that were never in your contract. Then we win that money back and ensure fair pricing going forward.

Garbage

Garbage invoices are messy on purpose, and most companies overpay by 10 to 30 percent without ever knowing. We check whether your bins are too big or picked up too often, catch unallowed fuel and environmental fees, and ensure the garbage hauler is held accountable for missed pickups.

Construction

We go through the construction invoices line by line. We make sure labor is billed at the rate your contract says, that the work actually got done, and that every change order was approved and priced correctly. We also catch double charges, marked up materials, and credits you were owed but never got.

Freight

One shipment can carry a base rate, a fuel surcharge, and a stack of extra fees, all pulled from different rate sheets. That leaves plenty of room for mistakes, and studies show 3 to 8 percent of freight spend is lost to them.

Across all of our services, clients generally save between 25 and 55 percent.
