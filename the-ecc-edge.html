The ECC Edge

The tools behind our savings

A single month of invoices can hold hundreds of thousands of lines. A spreadsheet cannot catch what hides in there. We can. The ECC Edge comes down to two things: software we built ourselves, and people who follow through.

Custom Software

  • First, we pull your invoices straight from vendor portals
  • If a bill is a PDF, our software reads it and turns it into data
  • All of it lands in our own database, clean and sorted
  • Then our software checks every single line against your contract
  • AI helps us move fast and catch errors a person would miss

Customer Service

  • We stay on your accounts the whole time we work together
  • We watch for new errors and make sure your pricing holds
  • We make the calls and sit on hold so you never have to
  • We push every change through until it is done
  • And the savings show up where they should, on your bill
